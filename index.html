Why should I use Kennel Connect?

Kennel Connect is a great way to adopt your next pet in a way that's easy for you! Just choose what kind of pet you're looking for and how far you're willing to travel for your pet, we will do the rest!

How does it work?

You'll start by making an account. Our site will walk you through how to fill everything out. Then, swipe away! We'll send you pets in your area that you can pick from. Once you've swiped right/picked your pet, the adoption centers will look at your profile to see if you're a good match for the pet! If you're the right match, the adoption center will message you to finish up the process! Your furry friends await!
