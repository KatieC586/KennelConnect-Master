About Us!

The one place to find your new best friend!

Who are we?

Kennel Connect was founded in 2023. It was created by Frida, Katie and Ruth. They have all done this project to make sure animals will safely find their new family and be loved and cared for. With the use of this new technology you will be able to swipe left and or right, costumize your settings and be able to find your new best friend in a small amount of time and be able to communicate with the shelters for extra information and pictures.
